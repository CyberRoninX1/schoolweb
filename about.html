About Our School

Learn about our history, mission, and values

Our History

Mahawilacchiya National School was established in 1965 with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Mahawilacchiya and surrounding areas. Over the past 58 years, we have grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

From humble beginnings with just 50 students and 5 teachers, we now serve over 2,500 students with a dedicated faculty of 120+ teachers. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has made us a beacon of education in the community.

Our Mission

To nurture young minds with knowledge, values, and skills, empowering them to become responsible citizens and future leaders who contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Core Values

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in everything we do.

Respect

Treating everyone with dignity and kindness.

Innovation

Embracing new ideas and creative approaches to learning.

Integrity

Upholding honesty and strong moral principles.

Community

Building strong bonds between students, parents, and staff.
