Admissions

Join our community of learners

Admission Process

1

Submit Application

Fill out the online application form or download and submit a printed copy to the school office.

2

Document Submission

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and photographs.

3

Assessment

Students will undergo an entrance assessment (for Grades 1-11) to determine placement.

4

Interview

Parents and students will have an interview with the admissions committee.

5

Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete enrollment forms and pay the registration fee.

Required Documents

Birth Certificate

Previous School Records

Report cards and leaving certificate from previous school

Immunization records and medical certificate

Parent ID
